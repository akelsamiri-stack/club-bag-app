Also check: ITTF logo + supplier printed on the topsheet, one side black, the other an approved colour (red, green, blue, pink or violet), surface flat and undamaged.
Serve clipped the net and landed in ›
Let — replay the serve. No limit on how many times. If it clips the net and misses the table, it’s your opponent’s point.
Ball hit the edge of the table ›
Point stands if it touched the top edge of the playing surface. Hit the side of the table below the edge? No good — point to the other player.
How high does the serve toss have to be? ›
At least 16 cm (about a ball can and a half), thrown near-vertically from a flat open palm, from behind the end line and above table height. No serving straight out of the fingers.
They touched the table with their free hand ›
Point to you — if it happens during a rally, touching the playing surface with the free hand (or moving the table) loses the point.
They hit it before it bounced ›
Point to you. Hitting or being struck by the ball over or moving toward the playing surface before it bounces (obstruction) loses the point.
Who serves at deuce? ›
One serve each from 10-10, alternating every point until someone leads by two. Twos, then ones. It’s the whole name of the shop.
When do we change ends? ›
After every game, and in the deciding game when the first player reaches 5.
Can I put my racket down / wipe sweat? ›
Towel every 6 points (and at change of ends in the last game). Each player also gets one timeout of up to 60 seconds per match.
